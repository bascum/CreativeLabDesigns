Our Story

Chris Pratt and Tyler Lemmond have over 25 years combined experience in education. This experience, partnered with extensive work in the areas of digital creation including web design, branding, podcasting, and video creation, has helped them unite the two fields to create a truly unique business model.


After working for years in the world of performing arts, leading large organizations, and creating high-energy and entertaining live performances, they are now bringing their creativity to the world of digital design to empower small business and entrepreneurs to build a brand like no other.


The key to their success in the classroom was relationships. This is often overlooked in the corporate/business sector. Through their creativity and original strategies, they are igniting a movement of relationship-driven commerce where every small business has a platform to tell their story.

Our Vision

Creative Lab Designs uses the power of creativity and storytelling to re-establish the human connection (relationships) in building small businesses.

Our Mission

Creative Lab Designs helps small business and entrepreneurs strategically and creatively tell their story to build relationships that develop into business success.
